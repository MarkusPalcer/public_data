The mods I used for Kitty Plays Morrowind

These are all the mods I have installed for Kitty Plays Morrowind:
Name Link Short description
Morrowind Code Patch Fixes some bugs and changes some of the behavior in Morrowind for better playability.
Required by some of the other mods.
Morrowind Graphics Extender XE Adds shaders and other graphics extensions
Required by some of the other mods.
Arukinns Better Books and Scrolls Replaces textures of scrolls and books.
Balmora Underworld Adds a neat little underworks area to Balmora.
Better Bodies Changes the body models and textures with higher quality ones.
I'm using the NSFW version with visible naughty bits.
Better Beasts Better Bodies for Argonians and Khajiit.
I installed all optional files except for the "Tails Fix"
Better Heads 2 Quality update to the faces
Better Clothes Quality update to the clothing
Bitter Coast Sounds Plugin Adds atmospheric sounds and flies to the Bitter coast
Correct Meshes Tweaks the UV-Maps of models
Correct UV Rocks Tweaks the UV-Maps of rocks
Dunmer Lanterns Replacer Replaces the paper lanterns with higher detailed ones.
Ebonheart Underworks Adds a sewer system to Ebonheart
Epic Balmora Adds dummy houses in the distance to Balmora, so it appears bigger.
Expanded Sounds Adds a lot of sound effects to the world.
Expansion Delay Delays the assassin attacks to when you're later in the main quest as to fit the lore of the expansions.
Glow in the Dahrk Illuminates the windows at night.
Graphic Herbalism Picking up plants changes their appearance in the world.
Graphic Herbalism Picking up plants changes their appearance in the world.
I am using the mining add-on as well.
Seyda Neen Expanded Makes Seyda Neen look more town-like.
Jobasha's Rare Books Expanded Adds more books to the shelves in Jobasha's shop.
Keg Drip The taps on kegs drip slowly.
Mistify Adds mist to the Bitter Coast region
More Better Clothes Vol I - Vanilla Replaces even more clothes with better quality versions
Morrowind Enhanced Textures Replaces many textures with high quality versions
TM - New Gnisis Enhances the Town of Gnisis
New Suran Enhances the City of Suran
Real Signposts Replaces the signposts with versions that can be read from afar
Remiros' Groundcover A mod only for the LOD Generator of MGE-XE which causes grass to be added to the ground where it fits.
Sadrith Mora Expanded A bigger, better Sadrith Mora.
Septim Gold and Dwemer Dumacs Graphics upgrade of the coins
Transporter Lights All travel agents equip lights at night time for deeper immersion
Vos Expansion Adds a lot more to the city of Vos
